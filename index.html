THE HIERARCHY OF DANGER

PAVAN TEJA TALLAPALLI | EMAIL:ptallap2@asu.edu

This interactive topography visualizes the Minecraft ecosystem across three dimensions. Marks: Isometric sprites representing individual mobs and an area chart representing the terrain. Channels: Vertical Position (Y) and Terrain Height represent the calculated Danger Score (Health + Damage * 3) and the danger score is only calculated for hostile and provocable mobs.Horizontal Position (X) groups mobs by score. Interaction: Scrolling right reveals increasingly dangerous creatures; hovering reveals detailed stats.

Custom Visualization Category: Combinatorial / Novel

This exhibit is considered Novel because i tried to creates a custom "landscape" metaphor where the data (Danger Score) directly generates the terrain geometry using a procedural area generator, i also tried to make the terrain animation as close to the in game dimentions and also tried to make the colors match that of the game's rather than using a standard chart type. It is also Combinatorial as it integrates isometric pixel art assets directly into the D3 coordinate system, combining a statistical scatterplot layout with a scenic illustration.

Attributes Visualized: Dimension, Danger Score, Health, Damage, Behavior Type.
